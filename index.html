What is a Black hole?

  • Black hole is a solution to the Einstein equation.
  • Has a horizon at the Schwarzschild radius ($\approx 3.8\times 10^{-7}$ pc in the Galactic Center)
  • Nothing can escape from this region!

    What is an X--ray binary?

    • Black hole (BH) accretes matter from star
    • Small BHs (~10 times the mass of the sun)

    X--ray binaries in the Galactic Center.

    Formation

    • BH XRBs / $\mathrm{M_*}$ $\sim 10^3$ times the rest of the Galaxy
    • These sources likely formed dynamically!
